Welcome to Another Universe

Origin is a future-focused IT company founded in Bangladesh with a mission to gain global recognition through innovation and impactful work. Origin aims to empower talented individuals—especially students—from Bangladesh and other Asian countries by offering meaningful internship opportunities and skill-based job offers.

The purpose of Origin is not only to contribute to the global tech industry but also to combat unemployment and unlock the potential of brilliant young minds. With a strong foundation in Computer Science and a commitment to building international bridges, Origin strives to be a launchpad for change-makers of tomorrow.

We envision a world where talent from every corner of Asia gets the platform to shine, collaborate, and contribute to real-world solutions that matter.
